Black vs White Privacy Policy

This privacy policy discloses the privacy practices for Black vs White. This privacy policy applies solely to information collected by Black vs White.

Keystroke Information Collection, Use, and Sharing

We do not collect keystroke usage. No keystrokes are sent outside the Black vs White application.

Device Information

We collect anonymous crash information for debugging purposes. Crash information is only used for determining the cause(s) of a crash and used to help with fixing of crashes.
